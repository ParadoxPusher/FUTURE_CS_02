[HIGH] Too Good To Be True: Promises free $500 gift card - unrealistic offer 
       you didn't enter or earn

[HIGH] Suspicious Domain: 'netflix-gift-center.com' is not an official 
       Netflix domain

[HIGH] Suspicious Link: URL points to unofficial domain

[MEDIUM] Urgency Tactics: Creates false scarcity - "expires in 48 hours"

[HIGH] Email Authentication: DKIM and SPF checks both FAILED

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ATTACK EXPLANATION:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This is a classic "too good to be true" scam. Victims are lured by the promise 
of a free gift card and tricked into clicking a link that either harvests 
credentials or installs malware. The urgency ("48 hours") pressures victims 
to act without thinking.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
REMEDIATION:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. If it sounds too good to be true, it IS too good to be true
2. Legitimate companies don't give away large prizes to random people
3. NEVER click links in prize/lottery emails
4. Delete immediately without responding
5. Report to your email provider as spam/phishing
