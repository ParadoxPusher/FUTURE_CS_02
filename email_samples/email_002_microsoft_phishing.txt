SUBJECT: Action Required: Password Expiration Notice

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Hello,

Your Microsoft 365 password will expire in 24 hours.

To avoid interruption to your email and services, you must update your 
password immediately.

Click here to update your password:
https://microsoft-365-login.secure-portal.net/password-reset

Note: Failure to update your password will result in account lockout.

Thank you,
IT Support Team
Microsoft 365

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

X-Mailer: Microsoft Outlook Express 6.00.2900.5931
DKIM-Status: NONE
SPF-Status: FAIL

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PHISHING INDICATORS IDENTIFIED:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[HIGH] Suspicious Domain: 'microsoft365-security-update.com' is not an 
       official Microsoft domain

[HIGH] Suspicious Link: URL points to 'microsoft-365-login.secure-portal.net' 
       - not microsoft.com

[MEDIUM] Urgency Tactics: Creates false urgency - "24 hours", "account lockout"

[HIGH] Email Authentication: No DKIM signature, SPF check FAILED

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ATTACK EXPLANATION:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

This phishing email impersonates Microsoft 365 IT support, using the common 
fear of password expiration to trick employees into clicking a malicious link. 
The fake login page would harvest corporate credentials, potentially 
compromising the entire organization.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
REMEDIATION:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

1. Corporate IT will NEVER ask you to reset passwords via email links
2. Always use your company's official password portal
3. Contact IT support directly if you receive password expiration notices
4. Report suspicious emails to your security team immediately
